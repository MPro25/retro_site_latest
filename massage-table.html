Massage Table

The SierraComfort Basic Portable Massage Table offers a sturdy 2-inch foam-padded surface, adjustable height, and a 450-lb capacity. Lightweight at 29 lbs, it folds for easy transport with a carry case. Includes a face cradle and armrests for comfort, making it a reliable choice for home or professional massage use on a budget.

Pros:

  • Easy to set up and fold.
  • Comfortable padding for price.
  • Lightweight with carry case.

Cons:

  • May wobble with heavier clients.
  • Face cradle feels flimsy.
  • Padding thinner than premium models.
